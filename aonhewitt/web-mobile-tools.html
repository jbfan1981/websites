Online Tools

SECURE MEMBER PORTAL

View Your Account Information Online

As soon as you get your member ID card, you can log in to Blue Access for MembersSM – your online resource for managing your health care coverage – to:

  • Check claims status and review Explanation of Benefits statements
  • Find a doctor or hospital using Provider Finder®
  • Estimate costs for tests, treatments and procedures

If you are already a member and haven't registered for Blue Access for Members, sign up today.

Provider Finder®

As a Blue Cross and Blue Shield (BCBS) Plan member you have the freedom to choose doctors and hospitals that best suit you and your family. Search our large network of doctors, dentists, hospitals and other health providers with our Provider Finder.

Care on Target®

If you have been diagnosed with a health issue, or you are worried that you are at risk for a health issue, take a survey to see how that issue might be affecting you. You will have the chance to learn more about that health issue and to get help for living with that issue from doctors and nurses. To access Care onTarget, log in to Blue Access for Members.

Well on Target®

Get the tools and support you need, plus rewards for making healthy choices. Gain access to a convenient, members-only website that includes a health assessment, interactive tools and educational information and tips, as well as a points reward program. Watch the video for more details.

To learn more, log in to Blue Access for Members.

Text Alerts & Reminders

Register your mobile phone number with us through Blue Access for Members to get important account information delivered to you via text messaging.** We'll text you information such as your:

  • ID card number
  • CoPay
  • Deductible

In addition, you can sign up for Health Tips by Text. These include:

  • Reminders to check your blood sugar, take your medication, or take your multivitamin
  • Diabetes diet tips
  • Exercise and fitness tips
  • Heart-healthy diet and basic care tips

Log in to Blue Access for Members to register.

** Supported carriers for text messaging: AT&T, Sprint, Nextel, Boost, Verizon Wireless, U.S. Cellular®, T-Mobile®, Cincinnati Bell, Alltel, Virgin Mobile USA, Cellular South, Unicel, Centennial Wireless and nTelos.
