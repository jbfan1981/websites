Advantages of Membership

There are many reasons why nearly 1 in 3 Americans have chosen Blue Cross and Blue Shield (BCBS) Plans. Whether you are a current member or considering switching to a BCBS Plan, now's the time to get familiar with the advantages of membership.

LargeNetwork

BCBS Plans provide access to a wide network of doctors and hospitals across the country and around the world, which makes it easier for you to get the care you need while saving money.

MemberServices

As a BCBS member, you have access to wellness programs, tools and resources to help you manage your health. These include:

Blue365® Member Discount Program*

Blue365 is an online destination for health-focused discounts. With discounts on health-related products as well as health and fitness clubs and weight-loss programs and so much more, you can decide what choices are right for you while saving money. Visit Blue365.

Fitness Program**

The Fitness Program membership is your affordable passport to fitness. This program offers access to a nationwide network of independently contracted fitness centers for a low one-time $25 enrollment fee and $25 per member per month fee, plus applicable taxes, available exclusively to you and your covered dependents (age 18 or older). Log in to Blue Access for MembersSM to register.

Lifestyle Management Programs

Call 877.217.7986 to get a personal health coach who can help you lose weight, quit smoking and reduce your risk for heart disease, stroke and diabetes. These programs produce outstanding health benefits to our members:

  • 63% of participants lost an average of 6% of their body weight
  • 29% of participants reported that they quit smoking

Web and Mobile Tools

Get the most out of your health insurance plan with our web-based tools and apps. Get started.
