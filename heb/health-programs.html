Condition Management Programs

If you have asthma, diabetes or another chronic condition – or are at risk for one – these programs offer education, coaching and monitoring with help from registered nurses and other health care professionals. Take action today to manage your health at no additional cost.

For more information, contact your Partner Care Team or visit Healthy at H-E-B

Tobacco Cessation

If you’re ready to stop using tobacco products, a lifestyle management coach can help you set goals, check your progress and provide support. This program is offered at no additional cost for all medical–enrolled Partners and their spouses and dependents ages 18 and older. Call 866-432-7289 to get started today!

Special Beginnings®

Available at no additional cost, this maternity program provides expectant mothers with education and support through early pregnancy until six weeks after birth. Learn more about Special Beginnings, or call 888-421-7781 to sign up.
