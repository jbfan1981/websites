Take Action in 2016

Through the Healthy at H-E-B incentive, you can save up to $400 off your 2017 medical insurance premium by taking small steps, such as completing a Partner screening each year and taking action on any out of range screening results. Check your progress on the Healthy at H-E-B  page on PartnerNet to see if you need to take action. If all your screening results are in-range or borderline, then you have automatically earned the savings. If any results are out-of-range, you simply need to complete ONE free health program from the choices below before October 31, 2016 so you can earn your savings. Here are a few of your options:

Health Classes

BCBSTX offers the following health programs:

  • Diabetes Education Classes — Call the Partner Care Team at 866-432-7289
  • Pharmacy Blood Pressure Classes — Call the Partner Care Team at 866-432-7289
  • Weigh-to-Wellness Classes — Call the Partner Care Team at 866-432-7289

Visit Healthy at H-E-B  page on PartnerNet for class requirements, schedules, and registration.

Registered Dietician Consulting

Registered Dieticians are available to help you assess your nutritional needs, set dietary goals, and recommend a meal plan to fit your specific tastes and preferences. Call the Partner Care Team at 866-432-7289 for more information.

Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Texas (BCBSTX) Coaching

BCBSTX offers the following programs for individuals with these conditions:

  • Diabetes: Call the Partner Care Team at 866-432-7289.
  • Asthma: Call the Partner Care Team at 866-432-7289.
  • Metabolic Syndrome: Call the Partner Care Team at 866-432-7289.

Tobacco Cessation Program

If you're ready to stop using tobacco products, let us help you reach your goal. When you quit, not only will you live healthier, you'll save money too. H-E-B will remove the $15 weekly tobacco surcharge for medically-covered Partners and their spouses over the age of 18 who complete this program.

Call 866-432-7289 to get started today!

Work With Your Doctor

Partners can also work with their health care provider on an alternative program. Visit Healthy at H-E-B  for more information on how to submit your form from your health care provider.

 

Once you've completed a health program, see your savings add up! Visit Healthy at H-E-B  for complete details on the new Healthy at H-E-B incentive.

H-E-B is committed to helping you achieve and maintain your best health. The premium incentive is available to all Partners enrolled in the H-E-B HealthShare plan. You can earn the incentive through activity and outcome-based options, participatory options, or a combination. The participatory options are available to everyone regardless of your health status or whether you are able to complete the activity or outcome-based options. If you think you might be unable to meet the tobacco-free standard, H-E-B will work with you (and, if you wish, with your doctor) to come up with a reasonable alternative that is right for you to earn the same reward. If you have any questions, contact H-E-B at HealthyatHEB@LiveHealthier.com or call 800-597-0653.
