Condition Management

If you have a chronic condition or are at risk for one, this program offers you education, coaching and help in monitoring your condition. Your Family Health Assistant can connect you to registered nurses or other health care professionals that will help you:

  • Identify unhealthy behaviors
  • Set wellness goals
  • Adopt healthier habits and
  • Learn to manage medical conditions more effectively

For more information, please visit Healthy at H-E-B

Lifestyle Management Programs

Get a personal health coach who can help you lose weight, quit smoking and reduce your risk for heart disease, stroke and diabetes.

These programs produce outstanding health benefits to our Partners:

  • 63% of participants lost an average of 6% of their body weight
  • 29% of participants reported that they quit smoking

To take advantage of these programs call 866-412-8795.
