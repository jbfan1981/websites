The Partner Care Team

Call the Partner Care Team before you seek care.

Call 886-432-7289 Monday - Friday, from 8 a.m. to 6 p.m.

The Partner Care Team can help you:

  • Find network providers
  • Schedule medical appointments
  • Get help with your benefits, claims or medical billing issues
  • Find health care programs and resources
  • And much more.

Family Health Navigators

Facing a difficult health issue and have to go to the hospital? Your BCBSTX Family Health Navigator is a registered nurse who can help coordinate the care you might need. Call 866-432-7289.

24/7 Nurseline

The 24/7 Nurseline is staffed by registered nurses who can answer many of your general health questions and advise you to call a doctor, visit an urgent care center or other facility, or seek emergency care services if necessary. Call the 24/7 Nurseline at 888-838-9473 anytime of the day.
